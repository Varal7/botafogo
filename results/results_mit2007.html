Visiting Team Standings



This year, the MIT Open gave recognition to the visiting school with the highest number of cumulative points based on the sum of points from placement in finals. Points are awarded to the schools for both the leader and the follower as follows: 10pts for 1st, 8 for 2nd, 6 for 3rd, 4 for 4th, 2 for 5th, and 1 for 6th.

1. 365 points: New York University
